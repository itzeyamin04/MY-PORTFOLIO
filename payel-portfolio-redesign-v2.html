A student of the space
between disciplines.

My academic path follows the discipline of computer information systems at BMCC — but my curiosity has never stayed neatly in one lane. I build web experiences with the precision of an engineer and the eye of a photographer. I wrangle data by day and chase light by evening.

The grid and the glow. The code and the composition. I want both.

I grew up fascinated by how systems work — how small pieces combine to create something larger than themselves. That curiosity has carried into web development, where every tag, every query, every pixel contributes to a whole. It's carried into photography, where light, moment, and framing do the same.

My long-term vision is entrepreneurship — building a technology-driven business from the ground up, where design thinking and technical craft aren't separate departments but one shared language. Every project I ship now is a brick in that foundation.
